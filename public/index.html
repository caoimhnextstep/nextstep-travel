Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking with NextStepTravel

You can cancel your booking free of charge up to 24 hours before your scheduled check-in date. Cancellations made within 24 hours of check-in may be subject to a fee equivalent to one night's stay. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to your original payment method.
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secured with 256-bit encryption. For bank transfers, please allow 2-3 business days for processing before your booking is confirmed.
Requirements vary by destination. Generally, you will need a valid passport with at least 6 months remaining validity. Some destinations may require a visa. We recommend checking the entry requirements for your specific destination during the booking process. You can upload copies of your documents when completing your booking.
Yes, you can modify your booking dates, traveler details, and special requests up to 48 hours before check-in at no extra charge. Changes to the destination or package type may incur a rebooking fee. Contact our 24/7 support team or use the "My Bookings" section to request changes.
Basic travel insurance covering trip cancellation and medical emergencies is included with all Complete Packages. For Vacation Packages and standalone bookings, travel insurance can be added during checkout for an additional fee. We strongly recommend travel insurance for all international trips.
